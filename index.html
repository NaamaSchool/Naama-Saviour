About Naama Saviour School

A beacon of hope and education in our rural community

Our School

Located in the heart of Sekyere East, Ashanti Region, Naama Saviour School serves as a cornerstone of education for our rural community, providing quality learning opportunities for children in the area.

150 Students

We proudly educate 150 students across both primary and junior high school levels, nurturing young minds and preparing them for a brighter future with dedicated teachers and community support.

Community Impact

As a village school, we are deeply rooted in our community, fostering not just academic excellence but also character development and community values that will serve our students throughout their lives.
